Our Story

Joy, Connection, Love

HappyPaws was created to make pet adoption process easier, safer and more accessible. The founder of the shelter is called Shari D. Coleman and he is a veterinarian expert. Our website allows users to discover animals, save their favourite pets that need support and adopt in two steps.

We also support local animal shelters in Greece and non-profit organizations by providing food for animals and financial support. Our shelter collaborates with an animal shelter located in Thessaloniki, Greece. In this way, all animals are being taken cared of.
